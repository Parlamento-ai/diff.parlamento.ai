Script: poc-akn-diff.ts Reglamento: Reg (EU) 2019/2088 — Sustainable Finance Disclosure (SFDR) V1: 02019R2088-20200712 (21 articles) V2: 02019R2088-20240109 (22 articles) Cambios: 1 insercion Archivo: changeset-2019-2088.xml (5.5 KB) Verificar EUR-Lex ↗

Caso de uso real

Se descargaron dos versiones consolidadas del mismo reglamento (SFDR) desde CELLAR: la version vigente al 12 julio 2020 y la version vigente al 9 enero 2024. El diff detecta automaticamente que cambio entre ambas.

La version 2024 incorpora las modificaciones del Reg (EU) 2023/2859 (European Single Access Point — ESAP).

Article 18a — Accessibility of information on the European single access point insertion
1. From 10 January 2028, when making public any information referred to in Article 3(1) and (2), Article 4(1), (3), (4) and (5), Articles 5(1) and 10(1) of this Regulation, financial market participants and financial advisers shall submit that information at the same time to the relevant collection body referred to in paragraph 3 of this Article for the purpose of making it accessible on the European single access point (ESAP) established under Regulation (EU) 2023/2859.
That information shall comply with the following requirements: (a) be submitted in a data extractable format as defined in Article 2, point (3), of Regulation (EU) 2023/2859 or, where required by Union law, in a machine-readable format; (b) be accompanied by metadata: (i) names of the financial market participant or financial adviser; (ii) legal entity identifier; (iii) size by category; (iv) type of information; (v) indication of personal data.
2. For the purposes of paragraph 1, point (b)(ii), financial market participants and financial advisers that are legal persons shall obtain a legal entity identifier.
3. By 9 January 2028, Member States shall designate at least one collection body as defined in Article 2, point (2), of Regulation (EU) 2023/2859 and notify ESMA thereof.
4. The ESAs, through the Joint Committee, shall develop draft implementing technical standards to specify: (a) any other metadata; (b) the structuring of data; (c) for which information a machine-readable format is required.
5. Where necessary, the ESAs shall adopt guidelines to ensure that the metadata submitted in accordance with paragraph 4 are correct.
